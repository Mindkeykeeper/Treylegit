History

THE ERA is a Footwork Dance Company from Chicago also specializing in media. Performing for local enthusiasts, international audiences and everyone in between, at venues like the Barbican in London, Womb in Tokyo, PS-1 MoMa in New York, Pitchfork music Festival in Chicago and Hellow Festival in Monterrey, Mexico. THE ERA was conceptualized in 2010 as a grassroots Footwork crew on the South Side, uniting Chicago's top Footworkers into a new crew. THE ERA is co-founders LITEBULB, Ste-lo, co-founder / media coordinator Chief Manny, DIPSET and P-Top, supported by personal managers Dee-Dee Stallworth and Dana Givens, Surefire agent Devin Landau, Japanese affiliate Weezy, and founding advisor / media Coordinator Wills Glasspiegel. THE ERA is based in Chicago.. we give unique footwork workshops, perform cameos in feature length films, concerts, develop stage performances, create films, teach and research footwork history, produce digital animations while contributing to all sorts of creative work in and outside of Chicago.
